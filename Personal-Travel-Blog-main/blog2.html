3 Days Gulmarg (Our recommendations for Top Places to visit in Gulmarg) - Gulmarg Holiday Plan 2023

Nestled in the heart of the stunning Himalayas, Gulmarg is a picturesque paradise located in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. With its lush green meadows, snow-capped peaks, and a charming alpine atmosphere, Gulmarg is a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. In this 3-day Gulmarg holiday plan for 2023, we will explore the top places to visit and the best experiences to savor in this enchanting hill station.

Day 1: Arrival in Gulmarg

As I arrived in Srinagar, the gateway to Gulmarg, the anticipation of the adventure that awaited me was palpable. A scenic drive of about 50 kilometers took me to Gulmarg, a journey that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan landscape. The fresh mountain air and the captivating beauty of Gulmarg immediately cast a spell on me.

Gulmarg Gondola Ride

My first stop was the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world. The ride to the top, affording panoramic views of the Pir Panjal range, was nothing short of magical. I could see the confluence of the Zanskar and Sheshnag rivers from this vantage point, and the sight was truly awe-inspiring.My first stop was the Gulmarg Gondola, one of the highest cable cars in the world. Gulmarg Golf Course

Gulmarg is famous for its picturesque golf course, and I couldn't resist visiting. Surrounded by tall pine trees and snow-capped mountains, it's a golfer's paradise. Even if you're not into golf, the lush greens and serene surroundings make it a perfect place for a leisurely stroll.

Day 2: Exploring the Meadows and the St. Mary's Church

The morning in Gulmarg was greeted with clear skies and the promise of a new day filled with adventure.

Gulmarg Meadows

I set out for a refreshing morning walk through the Gulmarg meadows. Surrounded by colorful flowers and bordered by thick pine forests, the meadows were a tranquil haven. If you visit in spring, you'll be greeted by a carpet of vibrant wildflowers in full bloom.

St. Mary's Church

For a touch of history and architecture, I visited the St. Mary's Church. This century-old church is a fine example of British colonial architecture and is open to visitors. The serene ambiance inside was a nice contrast to the excitement of the meadows.

Day 3: Visit to Apharwat Peak and Departure

The final day of my Gulmarg holiday was reserved for one of the most breathtaking experiences in the region.

Apharwat Peak

I took the gondola once more, this time heading to the Apharwat Peak, which stands at a height of 4,390 meters. As I ascended, I watched the world transform from meadows to pine forests to snow-covered landscapes. The peak offers mesmerizing views of the Himalayan range, and the sheer grandeur of the place left me speechless.I took the gondola once more, this time heading to the Apharwat Peak, which stands at a height of 4,390 meters. Departure from Gulmarg

After soaking in the majestic beauty of Apharwat Peak, it was time to bid adieu to Gulmarg. I left with a heart full of memories and a promise to return, for Gulmarg had indeed worked its magic on me.

In just three days, Gulmarg had charmed me with its natural beauty, adventure opportunities, and a rich history. This hill station in the Himalayas is a perfect getaway for those seeking an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply someone looking for tranquility amidst the mountains, Gulmarg in 2023 is a destination that won't disappoint.
